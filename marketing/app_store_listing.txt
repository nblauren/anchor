PROMOTIONAL TEXT
----------------
Meet people around you, even without internet. Anchor uses Bluetooth to connect you with nearby people on cruises, at festivals, and anywhere off the grid. 100% private, fully encrypted, no accounts required.


FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
Anchor is a social discovery app that works completely offline. Using Bluetooth mesh networking, you can find people nearby, chat, and share photos, all without WiFi or cell service.

No servers. No cloud. No data collection. Just you and the people around you.

DISCOVER PEOPLE NEARBY
Open Anchor and people around you appear automatically. Browse profiles in a grid or use the radar view to see who's close by. No swiping, just real discovery.

CHAT WITHOUT INTERNET
Send messages and photos directly to nearby people using Bluetooth. Every message is end-to-end encrypted using the Noise protocol. Your conversations never touch a server.

SHARE PHOTOS FAST
Photos transfer at high speed via Wi-Fi Direct. If that's not available, Anchor seamlessly falls back to Bluetooth. Either way, no internet needed.

DROP AN ANCHOR
Send a quick, playful ping to someone who catches your eye. It's a simple tap that says "I'm interested" without the pressure of a full message.

BUILT FOR CRUISES & FESTIVALS
Anchor was made for the LGBTQ+ community, specifically for gay cruises and queer festivals where internet is expensive or nonexistent. It handles crowded environments with ease and saves battery for long event days.

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
- No account required: no email, no phone number
- All data stays on your device
- End-to-end encryption on every message
- No tracking, no analytics, no ads
- Panic Mode: one-tap emergency data wipe

KEY FEATURES
- Bluetooth peer-to-peer discovery and messaging
- End-to-end encrypted chat (Noise protocol)
- Wi-Fi Direct photo transfer with Bluetooth fallback
- Drop Anchor: lightweight interest signals
- Mesh networking extends range through other users
- Radar view and grid view for discovery
- Battery saver mode
- Block users and manage privacy
- Works on crowded ships and festival grounds
- No internet, no servers, no accounts

IMPORTANT NOTE
For the best experience, keep Anchor open while exploring. iOS limits Bluetooth discovery when apps are in the background. You'll receive a notification when new people appear nearby.
