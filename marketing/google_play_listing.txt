FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
Anchor is a social discovery app built for places where the internet doesn't reach: cruise ships, music festivals, remote retreats, and anywhere off the grid.

Using Bluetooth mesh networking, Anchor lets you find and chat with people around you without WiFi, cell service, or any internet connection at all. Everything happens device-to-device. No servers. No cloud. No data collection.

Built by and for the LGBTQ+ community, Anchor was designed for gay cruises and queer festivals where meeting new people should be easy, even when the ship's WiFi costs $20/hour.

HOW IT WORKS

Open Anchor and nearby people appear automatically. Browse profiles, send a message, share photos, or drop an anchor to let someone know you're interested. It's that simple.

REAL PRIVACY, NOT JUST A PROMISE

Most apps say they care about privacy. Anchor proves it:

- No accounts or sign-ups required
- No data ever leaves your device
- No servers, no cloud storage, no tracking
- End-to-end encryption on every message
- Panic Mode instantly wipes all your data if needed

Your conversations exist only on your phone and the phone of the person you're talking to. When you delete them, they're gone for real.

DESIGNED FOR CRUISES & FESTIVALS

Anchor thrives where other apps fail:

- Works on cruise ships with no internet
- Handles crowded festivals with 50-100+ people nearby
- Adaptive Bluetooth scanning saves battery on long days
- Mesh networking extends your range through other Anchor users
- Battery saver mode keeps you going all day

SHARE PHOTOS AT FULL SPEED

Send photos using Wi-Fi Direct for fast transfers. No internet required. If Wi-Fi Direct isn't available, photos transfer automatically over Bluetooth. You don't have to think about it.

DROP AN ANCHOR

See someone interesting? Tap the anchor icon to send them a quick ping. It's a low-pressure way to say "hey, I noticed you" without writing a full message.

FEATURES
