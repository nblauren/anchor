SUBHEADLINE

Anchor connects you with nearby people using Bluetooth. On cruises, at festivals, and everywhere the internet doesn't reach. Private by design. No servers. No accounts. Just real connections.


KEY BENEFITS
------------

1. WORKS WITHOUT INTERNET
Anchor uses Bluetooth mesh networking to discover people and exchange messages. No WiFi, no cell service, no problem. Perfect for cruise ships, remote festivals, and off-grid adventures.

2. TRULY PRIVATE
Your data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no cloud, no accounts, and no tracking. Every message is end-to-end encrypted. This isn't a privacy policy. It's how the app is built.

3. MADE FOR LGBTQ+ COMMUNITY
Anchor was designed for gay cruises and queer festivals. Meet new people in spaces where you can be yourself, without worrying about who's watching or what data is being collected.


FEATURES
--------

Discover Nearby People
Open Anchor and profiles of people around you appear automatically. Browse in a grid layout or switch to radar view to see who's nearby spatially.

Chat Offline
Send text messages directly to anyone nearby. Messages travel over Bluetooth, are encrypted end-to-end, and never touch a server.

Share Photos
Send photos at high speed using Wi-Fi Direct. If Wi-Fi Direct isn't available, photos transfer seamlessly over Bluetooth. No internet involved.

Drop an Anchor
See someone interesting? Send them a quick anchor, a lightweight, playful way to show interest without writing a message.

Mesh Networking
Anchor's mesh network lets messages travel through other nearby users, extending your range beyond direct Bluetooth distance.

Battery Smart
Adaptive scanning and battery saver mode keep your phone going through long event days. Anchor adjusts its behavior based on how many people are around.

Emergency Panic Mode
One tap instantly wipes all data from the app: messages, photos, profile, everything. For when you need it.

FAQ
---

Q: Do I need internet to use Anchor?
A: No. Anchor works entirely over Bluetooth. You don't need WiFi, cell service, or any internet connection.

Q: How far does Bluetooth reach?
A: Typically 10-30 meters (30-100 feet) in open spaces. On a cruise ship, walls and metal structures can reduce range, but Anchor's mesh networking helps extend it through other users nearby.

Q: Is my data really private?
A: Yes. There are no servers. Your data is stored only on your device. Messages are end-to-end encrypted and go directly between phones. We couldn't access your data even if we wanted to. The infrastructure to do so simply doesn't exist.

Q: Do I need to create an account?
A: No. There's no sign-up, no email, no phone number. Just create a profile and start discovering people nearby.

Q: What happens to my messages when I delete them?
A: They're gone. Since there are no servers or backups, deleted messages are permanently erased from your device.

Q: Does it work on both iPhone and Android?
A: Yes. Anchor works on both iOS and Android, and people on different platforms can discover and chat with each other.

Q: How does photo sharing work without internet?
A: Photos transfer using Wi-Fi Direct for high-speed delivery. If Wi-Fi Direct isn't available, Anchor automatically falls back to Bluetooth transfer. Either way, no internet is involved.

Q: What is Panic Mode?
A: Panic Mode is an emergency feature that instantly wipes all data from the app: your profile, messages, photos, and encryption keys. It's there for your safety and peace of mind.

Q: How many people can I see at once?
A: Anchor is designed to handle 50-100+ people in range, making it perfect for cruise ships and large events. It automatically adjusts its scanning to handle crowded environments.

Q: Does Anchor drain my battery?
A: Anchor includes adaptive scanning and a battery saver mode. On a typical event day, it's designed to run without significantly impacting your battery life.
